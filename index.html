What's RSS?

The web, without the noise.

RSS is a simple, open standard that lets a website publish its latest posts as a feed. Subscribe to the sites you love — news, blogs, podcasts, even YouTube channels — and everything new lands in one place. No algorithm deciding what you see, no ads in the timeline, no account to create. You follow exactly who you choose, and you see everything they publish, newest first.

Screensaver

Your TV stays alive,
even when you're away.

When Marquee goes idle it becomes an ambient display — the latest headlines from your feeds drift slowly across the screen above a large, easy-to-read clock. Set one of your own photos as the backdrop from the iPhone app, and it becomes the kind of thing you'll happily leave running.

  • Live headlines from your own feeds, gently rotating
  • A large clock and date, readable across the room
  • Set a custom photo background from the companion app
  • Click the remote and you're right back where you left off

A reader you'll want to look at

Typography worthy of the big screen.

Articles are rendered natively — headings, pull quotes, code, lists and inline images all laid out for a TV, not crammed into a web view. Pick the look that suits the room and settle in.

  • Four typefaces: System, Serif, Avenir and Casual
  • Four themes: Dark, Black, Sepia and Light
  • Five text sizes, from Small to Huge
  • Inline images, plus audio and video playback

Speed Reading

One word at a time.
A whole article in minutes.

Engage speed-reading mode and the article streams to the center of the screen, one word at a time. Each word's Optimal Recognition Point is highlighted in amber and pinned to a fixed reticle, so your eyes never move — the part that normally eats most of your reading time.

  • Dial the pace from a calm 100 up to 900 words per minute
  • Swipe up or down on the remote to change speed live
  • Skip or rewind by sentence; pause to see the words around you
  • Smart pauses on commas and full stops keep meaning intact

Always up to date

Everything new, at a glance.

A dashboard greets you with what's unread, what arrived today, and how your feeds are doing. Marquee fetches politely in the background — honoring each feed's cache and back-off rules — so it's quietly current whenever you sit down.

  • Unread, Today, This Week and per-feed counts
  • Conditional fetches that respect each site's schedule
  • Mark all read, star, and jump between articles instantly
  • Open any story full-screen, or speed-read it in a tap

Marquee for iPhone

Your whole setup,
controlled from your pocket.

The free companion app is the easiest way to set up and tune Marquee. Manage feeds and folders, switch themes, typefaces and text sizes, and adjust the screensaver — including a custom photo background you can only choose here. Everything syncs to the TV over your own iCloud, instantly. And when a story deserves the commute, send it straight to your phone.

  • Control every TV setting from your phone — themes, type and more
  • Set a custom photo background for the screensaver — companion-only
  • Add, remove and organize feeds and folders, synced both ways
  • "Send to iPhone" pushes any story to a tidy reading inbox
  • No sign-up — it all rides on your own iCloud account
